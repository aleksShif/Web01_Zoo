Red Panda

The red panda is a small, tree-dwelling mammal native to the Eastern Himalayas and China. With its reddish-brown fur, bushy ringed tail, and masked face, it resembles a raccoon but is closely related to weasels and skunks. Primarily herbivorous, it feeds on bamboo, berries, and occasional small animals.
